Who We Are

Gerryke Slinn

CEO & Founder

Gerryke founded the East Ravenland Trading Company™ when he saw the need in connecting and investing in Ravenland following the end of the Blood Mist. Prior to founding his own company, Gerryke honed his business acumen over decades in a variety of roles including commodity trading, aggressive marketing, venture capital, personal relations, financial consultation and human resources. Having piloted the ERTC to success as the CEO, Gerryke is committed to utilising his experience and expertise to help bring prosperity to Ravenland. When given the chance, Gerryke enjoys exploring new cultures and exciting business ventures.

Snibble Flitwick

Co-Founder & Lead Asset Acquirer

Snibble has long had a passion and indisputable talent for determining, acquiring and critically leveraging the most valuable assets and opportunities in any given situation. A thoroughly motivated and highly talented asset accumulation, Snibble quickly realised the potential profits in combining his skills with Gerryke's marketing and vendor experience to form the East Ravenland Trading Company™, which to this day is still able to trade in only the most premium products thanks to Snibble's efforts. After hours, Snibble practices urban exploration, archery and sleight-of-hand tricks.

Othec

Logistics and Security Chief

As the ERTC has grown in both scope and selection, an expert in logistical transport and security became required, and Othec has confidently fulfilled these goals efficiently and effectively. With his background in freelance security and equine-based logistical fulfilment, Othec smoothly integrated the latest and greatest in equine logistic technology to significantly improve both trading capacity and speed. Alongside ensuring safe and rapid delivery of your goods, Othec also appreciates racing, dressage and spending time with his friend, Suncrest.

Rayden

Arcanical Consultant

Since the East Ravenland Trading Company™ has never been satisfied without growing into new frontiers and opportunities, it could never be restricted to merely mundane products. To confidently fulfil the growing need for premium arcane products and services, Rayden joined the company and has been passionately using his expertise to help us offer solutions and pioneering techniques in the range of options we can supply to our valued customers. In addition to conducting our corporate drug testing policy, Rayden has perfected his patience, dedication and scientific experimentation through his hobby of horticulture and apothecary studies.

Asher

Environmental Concerns Specialist

At the East Ravenland Trading Company™, we take the social responsability of diverse employment and environmental sustainablity seriously. It has therefore been a joy to welcome the enthuisastic and ecologicaly conscious Asher to our family here at ERTC. We're proud to be an ally to the wolfkin community, and Asher brings with him key talents in pathfinding our route to success; but rest assured, when a more human touch is required by our customer and client needs, Asher can take relaxation through long strolls enjoying nature and his people's traditional practice of solemn and solitary meditation.
